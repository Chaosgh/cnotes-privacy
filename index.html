CNotes Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-22

CNotes is a note-taking app that stores your content on your device. This policy explains what data CNotes uses and why.

Data We Collect

  • Notes data: text notes, checklists, kanban cards, flashcards, and app preferences.
  • Voice clips: recordings you choose to create in Voice notes.
  • Reminder metadata: reminder times and repeat settings used for notifications.

How Data Is Stored

  • Your notes and related data are stored locally on your device.
  • Voice clips are saved in the app's local storage area.
  • CNotes does not upload your data to a remote server by default.

Permissions

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): only used when you record voice clips.
  • Notifications: used to deliver reminder alerts you configure.
  • Exact alarms: used for reliable scheduled reminders on supported Android versions.

Data Sharing

CNotes does not sell your personal data and does not share your note content with third parties for advertising.

Export and Deletion

  • You can export notes using built-in export features.
  • You can delete notes and voice clips from inside the app.

Children's Privacy

CNotes is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us to request deletion.
