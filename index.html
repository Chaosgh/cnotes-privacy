CNotes Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-22

CNotes is a local-first note app. Your notes stay on your device unless you choose to export them yourself. This page explains exactly what CNotes stores and what permissions it uses.

What CNotes Stores

  • Note content: text notes, checklists, kanban boards, flashcards, and voice note metadata.
  • Voice clips: audio recordings you create in Voice mode.
  • Reminder settings: reminder dates/times and repeat options for your notes.
  • App preferences: local app settings used for normal app behavior.

Where Data Is Stored

  • Data is stored locally on your Android device.
  • Voice clips are stored in app-local storage.
  • CNotes does not require an account and does not run its own cloud backend.

Network and Sharing

  • CNotes does not sell personal data.
  • CNotes does not send your note content to third-party advertising services.
  • Data is only shared if you explicitly use export/share features provided by Android.

Permissions Used

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): used only while recording voice clips.
  • Notifications: used to show reminder notifications you configure.
  • Exact alarms: used so reminders trigger reliably at scheduled times.

Control and Deletion

  • You can edit or delete notes and clips inside the app.
  • You can clear trash and remove recordings from app storage.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove app-local data from the device.

Policy Updates

If CNotes privacy behavior changes, this page will be updated with a new effective date.
